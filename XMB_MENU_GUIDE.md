## Troubleshooting

**Q: Categories won't change?**
A: Make sure JavaScript is enabled. Try clicking directly on the icons.

**Q: Submenu doesn't show?**
A: Click on a category icon to activate it.

**Q: Page transitions are slow?**
A: Check your backend server is running (should be on port 5000).

**Q: Can't navigate with keyboard?**
A: Click anywhere on the page first to focus it, then use arrow keys.

**Q: Menu looks different than expected?**
A: Clear your browser cache (Ctrl+F5) and reload.
